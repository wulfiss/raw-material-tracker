# Product Context: Raw Material Tracker

## Problem
In food service and production environments, managing raw material freshness is critical for safety and quality. Manual tracking of expiration dates and material types is error-prone, often leads to waste, and is difficult to do efficiently while on the move (e.g., in a warehouse or kitchen).

## Solution
A digital Raw Material Tracker that centralizes material master data and streamlines the reception of new goods. By focusing on "expiration intelligence" and mobile-friendly data entry, the tool helps staff quickly identify at-risk inventory and record arrivals without needing a desktop computer.

## User Experience Goals
- **Efficiency:** Minimize typing during reception, especially on mobile.
- **Visibility:** Clearly highlight materials that are expiring or missing expiration data.
- **Reliability:** Maintain a single source of truth for materials to prevent duplicate or inconsistent entries.
- **Simplicity:** Use clear, actionable alerts (e.g., "Near Expiry") rather than raw date lists.

## Target User Personas
- **Warehouse/Kitchen Staff:** Need a quick way to log incoming goods (receptions) on a mobile device.
- **Quality Control/Managers:** Need to monitor expiration statuses and audit historical reception data.
